Programs

Progress with structured coaching

From foundations to advanced techniques, our programs are designed to help you climb stronger.

Beginner (4-week)

Foundations: tying in, belay basics, movement fundamentals.

  • Two coached sessions per week
  • Includes rental gear and safety certification
  • End-of-block assessment
Beginner

Intermediate (6-week)

Lead climbing, route-reading, fall practice, and efficiency.

  • Lead practice + clipping drills
  • Strength and endurance plans
  • Video review and personalized drills
Intermediate

Advanced (monthly workshops)

Competition tactics, board power, limit bouldering, and projecting.

  • 1–2 sessions per month
  • Mobility and injury-prevention
  • Guest setters and visiting coaches
Advanced
