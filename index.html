Climbing Areas

Multiple climbing zones designed for different styles and skill levels.

Rope Climbing Walls

Our main climbing area features 15-meter high walls with diverse, thoughtfully set routes. From beginner-friendly top-rope routes to challenging lead climbing paths, we offer experiences that match your skill level and push your boundaries.

  • 15-meter high climbing walls
  • Top rope and lead climbing routes
  • Auto-belay systems for solo climbing
Top Rope & Lead

Bouldering Zone

Our extensive bouldering area features over 200 square meters of climbing surface with problems thoughtfully graded from beginner to advanced, ensuring continuous challenge and growth.

  • 200+ square meters of bouldering
  • Two-story mezzanine for advanced routes
  • Crash pads and safety equipment
80+ Routes

Training Zone

Professional training equipment to build strength and improve your climbing performance.

2016 Moonboard

The original Moonboard featuring a standardized 40-degree angle and LED system, allowing you to train on the same benchmark problems as climbers worldwide.

  • 40-degree overhang angle
  • LED problem display system
  • Access to global problem database
Training

Campus Board

Traditional campus board for power training and contact strength development.

  • Multiple rung sizes
  • Progressive difficulty levels
  • Power and technique training
Training

Hangboard Area

Multiple hangboards for finger strength training and injury prevention.

  • Various grip types
  • Different hold depths
  • Finger strength progression
Training

Spray Wall

Large overhanging wall with random holds for creative problem solving and endurance training.

  • 30-degree overhang
  • Random hold placement
  • Endurance training
